The HiPEAC Vision provides a long-term (10-year +) view of trends in computing systems, and their impact on society. Issues discussed include:

  • What will the future web look like? How can we make it simple and safe for users?
  • What are the benefits and drawbacks of recent artificial intelligence (AI) advances?
  • What new technologies are on the horizon? How will these be integrated into computing systems?
  • What are the implications of poor cybersecurity and privacy safeguards? How can we protect users from cyberattacks and unwanted data sharing?
  • Why is sovereignty important for computing technologies and software? What should Europe do in this regard?
  • How can we make computing systems and devices more sustainable? How can digital technologies contribute to a more sustainable world?
